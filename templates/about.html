About Old Books Exchange

Our Mission

Old Books Exchange is a student-to-student platform designed to help college and university students save money on textbooks. We believe that quality education should be accessible without breaking the bank.

Our platform allows students to buy and sell used textbooks within their academic community, creating a sustainable and cost-effective solution for educational materials.

Why Use Old Books Exchange?
  • 💰 Save Money

    Buy textbooks at 40-70% off the original price from fellow students.

  • 🎓 Trusted Community

    Buy and sell only within your college/university community of verified students.

  • 📚 Wide Selection

    Browse thousands of books across all departments and semesters.

  • ♻️ Sustainable

    Reduce waste by giving textbooks a second life instead of buying new ones.

  • 📱 Easy to Use

    Simple registration, listing, and browsing process.

How It Works
  1. Register: Create an account with your college email and details.
  2. List Books: Upload your used textbooks with photos and descriptions.
  3. Browse: Search and filter books by department, semester, subject, and price.
  4. Connect: Contact sellers directly through the platform to arrange purchases.
  5. Trade: Meet up on campus or arrange convenient pickup/delivery.
  6. Mark as Sold: Update your listing status once the book is sold.
Safety & Trust
  • Verified Users: All users must register with their college details
  • Detailed Listings: Detailed book information and condition ratings help set expectations
  • Secure Transactions: Direct contact information for safe transactions
  • Community Standards: Honest sellers and buyers maintain platform integrity
